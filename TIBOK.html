Description

Singer and songwriter Earl Agustin returns with a music video of “Tibok" featuring Ashtine Olviga and Rabin Angeles from the popular *Ang Mutya ng Section E* Series. Earl Agustin channels pop, cool R&B and bits of tasty soul on this mid-tempo bop. Earl Agustin, through Vicor Music, brings back 70’s styled soul music through this catchy tune about falling in love.
