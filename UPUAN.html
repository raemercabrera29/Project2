Description

Gloc-9’s "Upuan", featuring the soulful voice of Jeazell Grutas, is a hard-hitting track that tackles the realities of corruption, privilege, and social injustice in the Philippines. With thought-provoking lyrics and a compelling message, Gloc-9 paints a vivid picture of life from opposite sides of the social divide. This lyric video allows listeners to follow the message word-for-word, highlighting the song’s poetic and powerful storytelling. A must-listen for fans of socially conscious Pinoy rap.
